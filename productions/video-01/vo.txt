## 04 falsifiable-card
A strategy must be written as exact, falsifiable rules before I can test it. When does a level count as swept? Which candle closes the order block? Ask ten ICT traders — you will receive ten different answers, each unfalsifiable in the moment and obvious in hindsight. If it cannot be written as code, I cannot test it. If I cannot test it, no one can disprove it. And a method that cannot be disproven is not a method. It is a horoscope with a Discord server.
